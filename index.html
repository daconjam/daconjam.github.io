About Me

Hi there! I am an incoming Tenure Track Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Morgan State University as of September 2023.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering from MSU in 2023 under the supervision of Dr. Jiliang Tang. Before that, I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science from MSU in 2020, and both B.S. and A.S. degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science, respectively, from the City University of New York—Medgar Evers College in 2018.

My research areas are Trustworthy AI, Natural Language Processing, Computational Social Science, and Interdisciplinary Data Science. Specifically, I focus on modeling social factors such as Race, Gender & Language in the development and deployment of new and innovative technology. Moreover, I regularly serves as PC member and reviewer for multiple top-tier international conferences and journals in machine learning and data science such as ACL, ACL-IJCNLP, NAACL, LREC, AAAI, KDD, WWW, and IJCAI.
