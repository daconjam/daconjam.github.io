From track star to doctor: Jamell Dacon’s Medgar Made journey

"A native of Trinidad and Tobago, he admits that navigating the complexities of higher education and the available resources to him was initially challenging. Add in a full course load, work, family responsibilities and the commitment that it takes to be an NCAA athlete — well it takes a special person to withstand it all." - Campus News (May 7th, 2024)

Dr. Jamell Dacon is a 2018 graduate of Medgar Evers College with majors in both Mathematics & Computer Science.

"Despite his busy schedule as an Assistant Professor and Senior Data Scientist at Morgan State University, Dr. Dacon remains connected to his alma mater through alumni events, volunteering, and mentoring opportunities. He continues to give back to the college community, sharing his expertise and experiences with current students."

Dr. Jamell Dacon's achievement as one of the first in the department's history to graduate with a doctoral degree exemplifies his exceptional dedication and scholarly prowess. His accomplishment not only underscores his commitment to academic excellence but also positions him as a trailblazer within the department, setting a precedent for future scholars to aspire to. Dacon's pioneering spirit and relentless pursuit of knowledge have undoubtedly left an indelible mark on the academic community, inspiring admiration and respect among peers and faculty alike.
