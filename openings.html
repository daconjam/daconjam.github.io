I am building an interdisciplinary lab, namely, Machine Intellence and Data Science (MINDS) Lab at Morgan State University. At the MINDS Lab, our primary research lies in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. I will be open to both co-advising and recruiting (Post-doc, graduate & undergraduate) students (Starting Fall 2024) for my research group with research interest in:

Trusworthy AI: Safety & Security, Robustness & Realiability, Responsibility, Explicability, and Well-Being

FATE in AI: Fairness, Accountability, Transparency and Ethics in AI

Social Good Applications: Information Disorder, Harmful Language Detection, and more.

AI+X: Education, Biology, Linguistics, Healthcare, Social Science and more.

Please take a look at my publications if you are interested my work, and this will also determine if the MINDS Lab is a good fit for you. I am determine to create a safe, diverse, inclusive, interactive and informative space with a goal of broadening diverse participation and representation, and amplify academic diversity. Be know that we understand that diversity has many facets, including race, ethnicity, gender, (academic) age, religion, disability, sexual orientation, socioeconomic status, cultural background, and intersections thereof. Therefore, if the above information resonates with you, please reach out at jamell dot dacon at morgan dot edu. Moreover, a formal recruiting posting will be made available in Summer 2024.
